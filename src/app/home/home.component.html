A hub for animal testing alternatives

Students, professors, researchers, doctors, pharmacists, veterinarians, and anyone interested in education, industry, military, tax funded institutes, private labs and any animal relating facilities can gather and share information to develop alternatives to animal testing around the world.

We create a humane curriculum for medical, pharmaceutical, bio, veterinarian education by offering the alternatives to animal testing.

We develop alternatives to animal testing in the lab with computer sensor, simulator, and artificial intelligence.

Government agencies in the USA, France, Germany, China and Russia cooperate for alternatives to animal testing development. The industries make the standard for animal testing alternatives.

We re-discover past data to prevent repeat tests. We input the information here, so we don't harm animals anymore.
